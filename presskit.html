Factsheet
  • Game name: Allegro
  • Developer/Publisher: Chapter One Studios
  • Genre: Precision Platformer
  • Platforms: Windows, Linux, macOS, HTML5, Android, iOS
  • Controls: Simple and accessible - either arrows, WASD, touchscreen, or a controller

Overview

Allegro brings a refreshing new twist to the game scene: a delightful burst of energy, with intriguing pathways and high-level challenges. It is a unique, hand-crafted, precision platformer, with both retro and modern elements, designed as games used to be long ago - minimalist, engaging, challenging and fun! Its creative puzzles are somewhat obscured by its minimalist artwork and dynamic physics. Lightning-fast re-spawns keep momentum and swift progression, whilst cool pathways open the door to a world of exhilarating speedruns. It is a discovery of inner strength, a reflection of how we can explore life’s stream of puzzles in search of our own solutions in today’s world.

Key Features
  • Instructions: Discovered purely by exploration, trial and error, defeat and success – thus enabling players of all ages and experience to get straight into the action, derive great accomplishment, and have a really fun and engaging adventure!
  • Level design: Intentionally encourages repeated trial and error, and learning from mistakes, before eventual success.
  • Music: Simple spritely tune for initial release, to be updated (when budget allows) with original and dynamic composition.
  • Original release: This first version of Allegro is complete; yet extensive expansions in content are already on the horizon, to be inspired by interest and feedback from a growing player base. Feature updates will include further acts, challenges and bonus levels, difficulty variants, time-based awards, original music composition, and more. . . as far as players might wish.
